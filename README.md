# Ribbit-Rush
Ribbit! Welcome to the magical world of lily pad pond! Your little frog desperately needs your help to reach back home by hopping across lily pads. But wait, it's not that simple. A challenge waits ahead! Your frog must use its brain or technically, your brain to guess the code word in order to hop forward without slipping into the pond.
YOUR MISSION : 
Help your frog reach its home at position 50 by carefully guessing correct words and 
helping it hop from lily pad to lily pad. But beware! Wrong guesses can slip your frog 
backwards:( 
HOW TO PLAY: 
You start at position 1. 
● You will be given clues to guess the code word which will have length between 
3-6 letters. 
● You will have 3 guesses to correctly guess each code word. Type in your answer 
and press enter. 
● If you guess it correctly within 3 guesses, your frog can happily hop forward the 
number of lily pads as the length of the word. 
● If you are not able to, then your frog slips back by number of lily pads or positions 
as the length of the code word. 
● Danger! - Game over rule - If you are on the lily pad where length of the code 
word is greater than the position you are at, failure to guess the word in 3 
guesses will lead to your frog slipping inside the pond. 
● TO WIN : reach the golden lily pad at position 50! 
Hop fast, think smart, and enjoy the Ribbit Rush!
